Focaccia with caramelized Onions, Cranberie and Brie

WOW your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. it will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

for thr focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a parking knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or fronzen cranberries

Method

  • Make the focaccia dough

in a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dougn ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. you don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporrate the last of the flour
